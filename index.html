Biography

Michael Jackson was born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana. He was the eighth of ten children in the Jackson family, a working-class family with a strong musical background. His father, Joseph Jackson, was a steel mill worker and a musician who recognized his children's talents early on. By the age of six, Michael joined his older brothers in a group called The Jackson 5, which quickly gained popularity.
